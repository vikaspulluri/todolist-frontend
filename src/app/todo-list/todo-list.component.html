Who's this app for?

What this app actually do?

It's a real time todolists application where you can make some friends and create a group task. You can add tasks as well as subtasks in a particular list and all your friends will be notified immediately. You can discuss the progress of task in a group chat window with all your friends. You have full previlieges over the lists/tasks/subtasks created by your friends also.

Where to start?

Lets make some friends first and this app best suited for group task completion. You can also start creating your own lists by clicking "+" symbol in the left sidebar and start creating tasks and subtasks.
